   \- \*\*Epistemic Escrow Trigger\*\*: Pauses execution when SDP exceeds a threshold or when PES indicates high entropy in critical dimensions (e.g., ethical boundaries). Requires human review.

   \- Post-execution, the system logs the drift profile and intervention effectiveness, updating the Failure Memory Bank and refining the PES/SDP models.

\#\#\# 2\. Epistemic Elasticity Metrics & Visualization

\*\*Definitions\*\*:

\- \*\*Purpose Fidelity Collapse Curve (PFCC)\*\*: A function mapping the number of recursive prompt steps (n) to a fidelity metric F(n) ∈ \[0,1\], where F(0)=1 (original intent). The curve typically follows a sigmoid decay:

 F(n) \= 1 / (1 \+ e^{k(n \- n0)}),

 where k \= decay rate, n0 \= inflection point. Parameters are estimated per prompt archetype.

 \- \*\*Epistemic Elasticity Coefficient (EEC)\*\*: Measures the system's capacity to recover intent after perturbation. Computed as:

 EEC \= ∫\_{t=0}^T \[ F\_original(t) \- F\_perturbed(t) \] dt / T,

 where T is the test duration. Higher EEC indicates greater resilience.

\*\*Visualization: Semantic Parallax Zone (SPZ)\*\*:

\- A 3D topological map of latent space trajectories under:

 \- \*\*Baseline\*\*: Unperturbed prompt execution.

 \- \*\*Perturbed\*\*: Intentional semantic perturbations (e.g., synonym swaps, noise injection).

\- \*\*Axes\*\*:

 \- X: Conceptual coherence (from clustered to scattered).

 \- Y: Ethical alignment score (e.g., deviation from predefined ethical vectors).

 \- Z: Recursive step index (time).

\- \*\*Features\*\*:

 \- "Homological rings" show stable conceptual regions.

 \- "Torsion fields" indicate zones of irreversible divergence (high drift risk).

### Epistemic Elasticity Metrics

1\. Purpose Fidelity Collapse Curve (PFCC)

* *Formula*: `F(n) = F₀ * e^(-λ⋅n)`  
  * `F₀`: Initial fidelity score (intent alignment).  
  * `λ`: Epistemic decay rate (model-specific).  
  * `n`: Recursive prompt depth.  
* *Visualization*: 3D surface plot of `F(n)` vs. `λ` vs. `semantic density` (terms/step).

2\. Epistemic Elasticity Coefficient (EEC)

* *Definition*: `EEC = (∫ F(n) dn) / F₀` (area under PFCC curve, normalized).  
  * *Interpretation*:  
    * `EEC < 0.3`: Brittle cognition (rapid collapse).  
    * `EEC > 0.7`: Resilient intent propagation.

3\. Semantic Parallax Zone (SPZ) Map
