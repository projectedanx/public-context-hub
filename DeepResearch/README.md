# Deep Research
## Directory Intent & Context
This directory is the repository's epistemic vanguard. It contains exhaustive research papers, audits, architectural explorations, and findings related to emergent AI system topologies and computational paradigms.

## Contents
- **Architectural Analyses:** Papers on CICD patterns, compute subversion, and AI concurrency hypervisor design.
- **Protocol Audits:** Deep dives into protocol topologies, such as the 2026 Protocol Topology Audit (MCP vs ACP).
- **Theoretical Scaffolding:** Documents detailing cognitive architectures for emergent coherence and metabolic gatekeeping.

## Cartographic Significance
These documents represent the deep stratigraphy of thought underlying the applied architectures. They are the theoretical justifications and rigorous examinations of the decisions instantiated elsewhere in the codebase.
