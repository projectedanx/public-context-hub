If meaning acts as a fluid, its motion must satisfy conservation laws analogous to fluid mechanics. We propose that the **Semantic Velocity Field**, $\\vec{v} \= \\nabla \\Psi$ (the gradient of semantic potential), satisfies a modified version of the **Navier-Stokes equations**.2

$$Re\_{sem} \= \\frac{V \\cdot L}{\\nu\_D}$$
