AI Governance for Data-Driven Democracy
1 source
This report proposes a strategic blueprint for integrating AI into democratic governance, focusing on Context Engineering 2.0 and the Context-to-Execution Pipeline (CxEP) to ensure AI systems are transparent, accountable, and equitable. It advocates for proactive equity through algorithmic reparation and decolonial AI, moving beyond simple bias mitigation. The framework emphasizes radical transparency using mechanistic interpretability and verifiable provenance via distributed ledgers to provide deep, auditable insights into AI decision-making. Furthermore, it redefines human oversight as symbiotic governance, employing "positive friction" in user interfaces to encourage thoughtful human intervention. Finally, the blueprint suggests decentralizing power through Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs) and Collective Constitutional AI (CCAI), alongside aspiring to intrinsic ethical guidance with advanced alignment techniques like ethical attractors and epistemic escrow.
