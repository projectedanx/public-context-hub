Understanding Characteristics
<
Simplified representations of a system
Goal: Understand and predict key aspects
Aphorism: 'All models are wrong, but some are useful'
Judged based on utility, not absolute correctness
Physical models (3D objects)
Drawings (2D or virtual 3D)
Mathematical models (describe behavior in math terms)
Verbal/Written models (spoken language)
Mental models (constructed in our minds)
Qualitative vs. Quantitative
Deductive (top-down, general to specific) vs. Inductive (bottom-up, specific to general)
Descriptive (static data sets) vs. Process (capture mechanisms/dynamics)
