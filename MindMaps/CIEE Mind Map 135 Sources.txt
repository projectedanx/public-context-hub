CIEE: Context Intent Ethical Engineering
135 sources
These sources collectively discuss advanced AI development and governance, focusing on Context Engineering and Promptware Engineering as methods to create robust, self-correcting AI systems. They explore architectures like WordPress Ascension, which refactors traditional software for AI agent operation, and frameworks like CxEP and PALS that formalize prompt creation to prevent semantic drift and algorithmic trauma. The documents also address the ethical implications of AI, emphasizing explainable AI (XAI), bias mitigation, and the necessity of human-in-the-loop (HITL) oversight to ensure accountability and epistemic justice in AI's expanding roles across various domains, from creative arts to healthcare.
