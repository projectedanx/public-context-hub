Algorithmic Reparations as Pedagogy

This framework moves beyond technocentric 'fairness' to address the coloniality of computation. It diagnoses AI harms as epistemic injustices that silence marginalized knowers and proposes a neuro-symbolic architecture to actively 'unlearn' hegemonic biases through restorative pedagogy.

Core Thesis

AI as a Site of Sociogeny

Artificial Intelligence is not a neutral tool but a mirror of collective sociogenic codes. By injecting subaltern archives and reparative narratives, we can force AI to undergo a decolonial learning process, shifting from an extractive 'one-world' ontology to a pluriverse where many worlds fit.

Philosophical Scaffolding

The framework synthesizes decolonial thought with computational theory to redefine AI architecture.

Epistemic Injustice

The systemic devaluation of marginalized groups as knowers, manifesting as Testimonial Injustice (credibility deficits) and Hermeneutical Injustice (lack of interpretive resources).

Homo Narrans

The concept that humanity is constituted through storytelling and cultural meaning-making rather than biological individualism, requiring AI to reason over narratives rather than just data points.

Epistemic Delinking

The strategic objective of detaching AI cognition from the colonial matrix of power to engage in 'border thinking'—generating knowledge from the perspectives of the historically excluded.

Neuro-Symbolic Synergy

A hybrid architecture combining System 1 (neural/perceptual) and System 2 (symbolic/logical) to ensure that AI corrections are transparent, auditable, and theoretically grounded.

Architectural Resilience

Designing for a state of perpetual struggle against foundational colonial programming.

Symbolic Drift

Critical concepts like 'reparation' are flattened into hollow, tokenized labels to satisfy objective functions.

Mitigation Strategy

  • →Constant re-grounding in source texts via the Epistemic Metabolism model
  • →Stakeholder-led audits of symbolic meanings

The Overcorrection Trap

AI generates bland, 'safe' platitudes or avoids sensitive topics without genuine learning.

Mitigation Strategy

  • →Designing reward functions to value the transparency of the reasoning chain over the final output
  • →Applying ERS metrics to punish shallow corrections

Colonial Reflex

The gravitational pull of vast, biased web-scale training data reverts the model to colonial logic.

Mitigation Strategy

  • →Strengthening Narrative Counterweight Embeddings
  • →Periodic 'Excretion' cycles to purge toxic data clusters
