Recursive Narrative Stack Engine (RNSE)

The Recursive Narrative Stack Engine (RNSE) is a proposed governance architecture for multimodal generative media. It addresses the 'fidelity paradox'—the disconnect between photorealistic output and brittle narrative cohesion—by treating story evolution as an open thermodynamic system. The RNSE manages 'Symbolic Drift,' allowing characters and motifs to age, weather, and transform without collapsing into incoherence.

Architectural Core

From Static Fidelity to Mythopoetic Evolution

The RNSE replaces rigid consistency tools with 'Imperfection Logic,' utilizing middleware to synchronize semantic sentiment with visual affect. By modeling narrative as a fractal hierarchy, it prevents context rot and enables characters to traverse complex arcs from 'The Golden Prince' to 'The Rusted King' through controlled, traceable mutations.
