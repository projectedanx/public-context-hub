The Pluriversal Epistemic Audit Agent (PEAA) Framework

The PEAA framework addresses the systemic risk of 'Epistemic Monoculture'—the convergence of AI outputs toward a narrow set of dominant Western, Educated, Industrialized, Rich, and Democratic (WEIRD) archetypes. By decoupling task execution from epistemic oversight, it creates a resilient ecosystem that protects human cognitive autonomy.

The Core Paradox

The Paradox of Algorithmic Creativity

LLMs demonstrate high fluency but limited genuine creativity, often relying on high-performance recombination of existing data. This process is governed by 'Latent Semiotic Gravity,' which pulls reasoning towards dominant statistical 'wells,' flattening cultural nuance and inducing long-term 'Cognitive Debt' in human users.

Core Theoretical Pillars

The framework utilizes complex conceptual blends to transform traditional security principles into epistemic safeguards.

Latent Semiotic Gravity

The phenomenon where high-dimensional latent space clusters (dominant archetypes) exert a gravitational pull on conversational trajectories, bending meanings toward familiar or culturally specific orbits and away from niche or indigenous perspectives.

Epistemic Escrow

A system-level safe mode that intercepts AI outputs violating 'Semantic Integrity Constraints.' It prevents potentially harmful or homogenized content from reaching the user, holding it for recursive auditing and repair.

Scar Tissue Archive (STA)

A persistent memory architecture that logs resolved epistemic failures as 'Symbolic Scars.' This archive informs 'Therapeutic Forgetting Gradient Calculus,' preventing the model from relearning harmful anti-patterns.

The Audit and Repair Cycle

The PEAA operates as a secondary oversight layer, ensuring that 'execution' is always mediated by 'audit'.

Step 1

Constraint Enforcement

The PEAA monitors the Executor Agent via the Model Context Protocol (MCP), applying Semantic Integrity Constraints (SICs) such as 'WesternGazeDominanceScore < 0.8'.

  • •Real-time monitoring of token trajectories
  • •Verification against pluriversal diversity quotas
Step 2

Recursive Contradiction Elaboration (RCE)

When a violation occurs, the output is held in Escrow. The PEAA prompts the Executor to analyze the source of its bias and propose alternative formulations.

  • •Neuro-symbolic reasoning dialogue
  • •Retrieval from non-WEIRD knowledge corpora
Step 3

Algorithmic Reparation

The Executor retrieves grounding information from a Pluriversal RAG system to revise its response, ensuring adherence to the violated constraint before release.
